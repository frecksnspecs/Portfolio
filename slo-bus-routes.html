Idea

Background

Public transportation in San Luis Obispo may not have the dazzling reputation of the subway system in New York, but it can be useful and reliable if you have the right resources. SLO’s former bus app felt like a relic of the 2000s with its confusing interface and limited functionality.

For Evernote’s Hackathon at Cal Poly, our team wanted to design an Android app that removes the guesswork from utilizing the bus system.

Goals

  • Intuitive and accessible
  • Don’t miss the bus
  • Make room for Murphy’s Law
